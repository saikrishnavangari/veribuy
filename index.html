Welcome to VeriBuy™ – A revolutionary anti-counterfeit track and trace and product information system.

Introduction

>
  • An interactive anti-counterfeit and product interaction technology
  • Provides authentication, validation and verification of products via a custom developed app
  • Uses a patented dual layer technology system made up of a pair of unique and highly secure product codes
  • Ensures consumers purchase a genuine product

The codes are made up of an overt (visible) and covert (invisible) code. The overt code can be scanned by consumers pre-purchase, while the covert code is hidden, and is revealed by peeling off the overt code, post-purchase.Scanning the overt code verifies the product is what it says it is.When the product is purchased, the buyer is at liberty to peel off the visible code to reveal a hidden code.

When this hidden code is scanned, another method of authentication is provided through the use of a verifier item This verifier item is a piece of information given that can only be verified by the purchaser. For example, a scoop with a green dot is inside a can of infant formula, and this unique object is stored as information on the VeriBuy app.

The underlying security principle behind VeriBuy™ is our “Product Security Trinity” – the concept that true product security and maximum functionality can only be achieved through a relationship of the 3 parties who are involved in consumerism. Each party plays a major role in functionality, benefiting and contributing to input and output of the process.

For Consumers

“Product counterfeit and fraud jeopardises public health and safety, poses a serious risk to national industries and undermines legitimate business.”

Consumers are at risk due to high prevalence of counterfeit products on the market today. Here are the facts:

  • 70% of all counterfeit goods come from China
  • Counterfeit goods are estimated to account for 2% of all international trade
  • Over $25 billion worth of counterfeit goods are imported into EU and the US annually
  • The US and EU only acknowledge counterfeiting as a ‘soft crime’, making it low on the law enforcement and trade policy priority list

At best you receive an inferior product, however at worst; the product is detrimental to your health. For this reason, VeriBuy™ provides:

  • Advanced product recall functionality, with direct links to consumer and retailer communication channels (currently unavailable in the marketplace)
  • An easy-to-use interface for product verification
  • Increased safety, confidence and trust of product quality

  • Covers a broad scope of product security and safety related issues
  • Allows for interactivity with the consumer both pre and post purchase
